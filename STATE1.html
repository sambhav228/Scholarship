1.National Scholarship for Persons with Disabilities 2017-18

->CLICK HERE

National Fellowship for Persons with Disabilities 2017-18 also known as the Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship (RGNF) is open for students with disabilities who have been admitted to M.Phil./Ph.D degree in a University or academic institution. The scholarship scheme aims to enable meritorious but disabled students to effectively take advantage of various opportunities at the national and international level. Under this scheme, the students will receive a monthly fellowship of up to INR 18,000 along with various other benefits.

Who is offering this scholarship ?

This scholarship is being offered by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.

Bharati Scheme for Education (BSE) for the academic year 2017-18, introduced by Andhra Pradesh Brahmin Welfare Corporation (ABC), is for the Brahmin students of Andhra Pradesh A specific amount shall be paid to the selected applicants as one-time financial assistance through the prescribed process under this scheme.To encourage Brahmin student, continue their education, by providing one time financial assistance .This scheme is applicable for Brahmin students of Andhra Pradesh covering all 13 districts, who are studying from 1st standard to Post Graduation in the academic year 2017-18 as per the eligibility criteria provided .he student and his/her parents should belong to Brahmin community and parents should reside in Andhra Pradesh Should have passed in all the subjects till previous year of study Must not be getting scholarships from other sources as well, except availing benefit from Archaka Welfare Trust Family annual income should be not exceeding INR 3 Lacs .

3.NTR Videshi Vidyadharana Scholarship Scheme for BC and EBC Students

->CLICK HERE

Under the scheme “AP NTR Videshi Vidyadharana” (also called the Overseas Sholarship For BC Students), BC Graduate candidates of Andhra Pradesh are provided with financial assistance for pursuing Post Graduate studies in foreign countries. The scheme is a boon to the BC candidates who do well in the studies but are not able to acquire studies abroad because of their financial status. Andhra Pradesh state BC welfare Development is giving Rs 10 lakh to the eligible BC candidate as a scholarship and other allowances which is non-repayable. This scholarship amount shall be used by the BC candidate for tuition and living expenses while studying overseas. Number of BC candidates that are selected for the scheme is 1000 per year. The scheme is applicable for all eligible BC Graduate candidates.For Post Graduate Course: degree in Engineering/ Management/ Pure Sciences/ Agriculture Sciences/ Medicine & Nursing/ Social Sciences/ Humanities. For PhD Course: P.G Course in Engineering/ Management/ Pure Sciences/ Agriculture Sciences/ Medicine/ Social Sciences/ Humanities.The maximum age for eligibility for the NTR Videshi Vidyadharana scheme is 35 years as on 1st July of the year of advertisement.Income from all the sources of the candidate’s parents/guardian is taken into consideration. This income so calculated should be less than Rs 6 lakh per annum. The income certificate must be obtained through MEE SEVA. If the candidate is an employee, a copy of the latest Tax Assessment and also latest salary slip is required to be enclosed with the application.Notification for applying for the scheme will be provided on university campuses and also in e-PASS portal.7: 10: 7 bifurcation ratio will be done between BC – A, B, D groups.BC candidates from 13 districts can apply for the scholarship. Willing candidates can apply from 1st August to 30th September of the year. 33% — reserved for female candidate in the scheme which can be transferred to male candidates if the number of female candidates are insufficient.

4.Vidyadhan – Andhra Intermediate Scholarship Program

->CLICK HERE

Vidyadhan Scholarship Program from Sarojini Damodaran Foundation supports the College Education of meritorious students from economically challenged families The students are selected after completion of 10th grade through a rigorous selection process. Those selected will be eligible for two-year scholarship from the Foundation.The scholarship amount for graduation courses varies from Rs 10,000 to Rs 60,000 per year depending on the course and duration The selected students will be required to attend the mentoring programs from the Foundation [The scholarship amount for 11th and 12th grade will be a maximum of Rs.6000/year].For 11th Grade Students whose family annual income is less than Rs.2 Lakhs and who have completed their 10th grade/SSLC exam in the year 2018 from Tamil Nadu or Puducherry. They should also have scored 90% or obtained A+ in their 10th Grade/SSLC examination. The cut off mark for students with disability is 75% For 12th Grade Students whose family annual income is less than Rs.2 Lakhs and who have completed their 12th Std in the year 2018 from Tamil Nadu or Puducherry. They should also have scored 90% in their 12th Std. The cut off mark for students with disability is 75% or good rank in the entrance test for Professional Courses.SDF will shortlist the applicants based on the academic performance and the information provided in the application form. The shortlisted candidates will be invited for a short test/interview Exact locations for the test and interview will be informed to the shortlisted students individually SDF will reimburse the out of district travel expense of the student and a parent for eligible cases

5.Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship, Andhra Pradesh

->CLICK HERE

Welfare department of CCB provides financial support and scholarship to the students in collaboration with Government approved educational institutions for obtaining higher education. Most of the students are not aware of these facilities & financial support provided by CCB, College Trust and Government of India and they miss the opportunity to get higher education. Under the scholarship scheme of CCB, students can apply for admission to the leading educational institutions in their reserved seats. 20% seats are reserved for SC, ST, OBC, General and Minority financial backward students. All CCB Associated colleges are approved by AICTE, UGC, MHRD-Govt. of India, State Government and other required department. To avail the benefit of this scholarship scheme, it is compulsory for the students to apply online on CCB website.First of all, Select your state and Read the matters carefully on your state page. Apply Online & Take printout of Application Receipt/Counselling Letter. (Mandatory) Attend the counselling process as mentioned in your counselling letter. Select your choice college from CCB colleges list which will be provided at the time of counselling. Take printout of Allotment Letter of your choice college from CCB and talk to your college director (contact number is given in the Allotment Letter). Report to your allotted college with the CCB Allotment Letter and do admission process under scholarship scheme within 5 days of your allotment. Inform your admission to CCB scholarship department by calling/whatsup/SMS on mobile number- 6202601616. You must save this number for any further assistance.
